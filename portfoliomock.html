I am a UX/UI designer with a love for music and crisps. Currently attending the Belfast School of Art. Welcome to my portfolio site showcasing all of my recent work, have a look around and let me know what you think!

Branding Myself

This year, I have been focused on building a brand. Who am I? How do I want to be thought of as a designer? What aspect of design do I want to focus on?

Through using my initials and based on my characteristics I have designed a monogram, wordmark and visual mark to represent me as a designer. These are accompanied with a set of professional style guidelines to ensure consistency throughout the brand.

Designer Essay/Website

There are hundreds of amazing designers in the world, but in my eyes, there are none more interesting and unpredictable than Stefan Sagmeister.

For this reason, this is who I chose to base my designer essay on. As part of the project, I was also tasked with coding the essay into a website to make it a bit more visually appealing. Take a look!

Travel App

Have you ever felt like a holiday could’ve had more potential? Or that the stereotypical recommendations just aren’t cutting it for you? You need something tailored for YOU!

This is why I have designed your very own avatar, TravelBud! TravelBud takes into account your interests and wants to recommend the best things to do specifically for YOU! so that you never have any holiday hindsights again.

Data Visualisation

Data visualisation can often be boring; colourless pages packed with monotonous statistics and information. I was tasked with designing an infographic poster that was illustrative and engaging. I created colourful icons and used concise explanations that were easy to read and understand.

I decided to base mine on the topic of knife crime in the UK. Take a look!
